About Kariuki Nicholas Gathogo

Kariuki Nicholas Gathogo is a passionate Full Stack Developer based in Kenya with expertise in modern web technologies including Vue.js, React, and Node.js.

With a background in computer science and several years of professional experience, Nicholas specializes in building responsive, user-friendly web applications. His journey in tech began at a young age, tinkering with computers and eventually pursuing formal education in software development.

When not coding, Nicholas enjoys contributing to open-source projects, mentoring aspiring developers, and exploring new technologies in the ever-evolving web development landscape.
